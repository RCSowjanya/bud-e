    How bud-e Works

    Get the App & Login

    Download and install the App on your phone. Login with Aadhaar Linked Phone Number.

    Find the bud-e

    Choose a bud-e from the available lot in your location. Check the geofence limits before you ride.

    Scan the QR Code

    Scan QR Code to unlock Bud-e and enjoy a hassle free ride.

    Pick Up

    A navigation is provided to reach your bike. You can locate the bud-e near by with hooter help.

    End Ride

    End your ride with click of a button and make payment

    Park it back

    Park bud-e at your convenient legal parking spot.

    Vijay Madduri

    Vijay is Co-Owner of Hyderabad ISL Football Team, Venture capitalist and a die-hard technologist at heart, Vijay comes with more than 25 years of diverse experience in thought leadership, business development, client services, delivery and people management. He was a co-founder of Incessant Technologies which had a successful exit. He now founded Aimlytics Technologies to provide next generation solutions through Artificial Intelligence. During this stint, he was quick to identify the challenges of urban mobility, chaotic congestion, wait time and dependency on shuttle services, and the challenges of shared transport services during the pandemic. Hence, the idea of bud-e was born. Vijay holds an MBA from University of Texas at Austin and an MS in Computer Science from University of North Texas.

A shrewd dealmaker and an extremely loyal, hardworking personality; he has always chosen to be an innovator and path breaker.

    As serial entrepreneur, promoter, and director at bud-e, Vamsi is responsible for the firm’s operations management, deal sourcing and financial modelling.     Vijay Devarakonda

    Devarakonda Vijay Sai is a Pan Indian film Actor and Producer. He made his debut in 2011 and is best known for his work in the Telugu Film Industry. The role that revolutionized his career was Arjun Reddy Deshmukh from the movie Arjun Reddy. Not only a versatile actor, Devarakonda is also a producer. He owns King of the Hill Entertainment, a film production company, which produced its maiden film Meeku Maathrame Cheptha in 2019. He was featured by Forbes India in their 30 Under 30 list and was the most searched South Indian actor according to Google’s annual report. His immense popularity and crowd-friendly nature had gotten him various endorsements and brand deals. He also launched his fashion brand Rowdy Wear. A man with a conscience and a heart, Vijay Deverakonda, now has chosen to associate himself with the Watts and Volts, a company that is bringing us closer to a greener and cleaner future with their range of sustainable and eco-friendly electric bikes.
